Director's Message

Wang Hui
A famous Chinese writer, scholar. Professor of Humanities, Tsinghua University. "New Left" leaders

"Higher Studies" is a new mechanism of academic research since World War II rise of the international academic community, and its purpose is to natural science, frontier research humanities and social sciences in various fields on the basis of, and advocate an interdisciplinary, cross-cultural and cross-national research and discussion of the most outstanding and the most promising scholars engaged in interdisciplinary research conditions, to break the traditional division of disciplines, open up a new academic growing point. American Institute for Advanced Study in Princeton Institute for Advanced Study in Berlin, Germany and is an example of the Higher Institute of the contemporary world, generating a large number of significant and far-reaching research. From a global perspective, the previous higher studies entirely in Europe and the United States as the center, although a small number of scholars invited to other regions, but the development of research programs and more to the humanities and social studies in Europe and the United States based. After the end of the Cold War, higher studies, especially for Advanced Study in the Humanities and Social Sciences began to important changes in the region and countries in Europe and outside the United States to establish their own mechanisms for higher studies to become an important trend. Mr. Yang Zhenning in natural science-based founder of the Center for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University, pioneered the study of the establishment of higher institutions of its kind, and the establishment of the Higher Institute of Tsinghua University, the humanities and social, not only for the creation of world-class universities, Tsinghua needed but in the long run, will effectively stimulate and promote the Chinese humanities and social science research, for the world of higher studies will also be an important contribution.

Humanities and Social Tsinghua University Institute of Advanced Studies is committed to creating the humanities and social sciences research a high-level international platform to explore and establish a new research model, the promotion of domestic humanities and social science research, facilitate the emergence of new disciplines to enable the Institute to become a major force in the world in the field of higher studies. In the historical conditions of globalization, in the form of higher studies to attract outstanding scholars of Humanities and Social Sciences of Tsinghua University to engage in academic research to the convening of an international conference, open seminars, and published research results. We warmly welcome the arrival of domestic and foreign outstanding scholars!
